Semester 9

Verifying the Union of Manifolds Hypothesis on Image Datasets

Reproduced and extended key experiments from Brown et al. (2023) to validate the Union of Manifolds Hypothesis (UoMH). Confirmed disconnected class supports and varying intrinsic dimensions using VAEs/WAEs and intrinsic dimension estimators on MNIST, Fashion-MNIST, SVHN, and CIFAR-10. Explored applications in classification, clustering with fewer components, and tested the hypothesis on audio data (AudioMNIST).
